Community Leader Toolkit
Your Community Counts on You

The Community Leader Toolkit was developed to support state-funded partners and community organizations. Use these materials to educate your community about prescription opioids, create social media content and blog posts, help people have successful conversations with loved ones and promote awareness of the Texas Targeted Opioid Response (TTOR) initiative.
